Project Overview

Understanding the Problem & Solution

The Problem

Manual classification of thousands of Arabic news articles is time-consuming and inconsistent. News organizations need automated systems to categorize content efficiently and accurately.

The Solution

An intelligent machine learning system that automatically classifies Arabic news articles into three categories: Sports (رياضة), Politics (سياسة), and Economy (اقتصاد) with high accuracy.

The Impact

Enables real-time content categorization, improves user experience through better content organization, and reduces manual labor costs by up to 95%.

Project Details

  • Dataset: Arabic news articles with labeled categories
  • Categories: 3 classes (Sports, Politics, Economy)
  • Preprocessing: Arabic-specific text normalization and cleaning
  • Feature Extraction: Bag of Words (CountVectorizer) and TF-IDF
  • Approach: Comparative analysis of classical ML algorithms
